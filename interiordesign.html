INTERIOR DESIGN PROJECT- STUDIO APPARTMENT

This project was part of a summer Interior Design course at CSVPA (Cambridge School of Visual & Performing Arts) , 2016.

During this course we studied the basics of illustration, colour theory principles, 3D modeling and interior-design styles.

In our final project we had to design an 60 sqm studio appartment.
